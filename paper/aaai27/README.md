# AAAI 2027 main-track submission

Scaffold for the AAAI 2027 paper described in
`paper/publication_plan.md`. The narrative source-of-truth remains
`paper/visualization-tools-putnam.md` (the long-form draft); this
directory holds the **compressed 7-page AAAI version** that will
actually be submitted.

## File layout

```
paper/aaai27/
├── README.md          (this file)
├── main.tex           (paper skeleton; sectioning frozen)
├── references.bib     (bibliography stub; add as you write)
├── figures/           (figures generated by the analysis scripts)
└── aaai-style/        (the AAAI 2027 style files; placeholder until release)
```

## Building

When the AAAI 2027 author kit is released (typically May–June of the
deadline year), drop its `.sty`, `.bst`, and template files into
`aaai-style/` and uncomment the `\usepackage{aaai27}` line at the
top of `main.tex`. Until then, `main.tex` is buildable with vanilla
`article.cls` for drafting purposes.

```
cd paper/aaai27
pdflatex main && bibtex main && pdflatex main && pdflatex main
```

## What goes in main.tex vs the long-form draft

| Long-form draft (`visualization-tools-putnam.md`) | AAAI version (`main.tex`) |
|---|---|
| 12 narrative sections, ~700 lines | 6 sections, ~7 pages |
| All 5 studies in full detail | Studies 6/7/8 are the spine; Studies 1–5 condensed to a single "preliminary observations" subsection with pointers to the appendix |
| Chronological "Study 1, then we noticed…" structure | Punch-line-first: discovery-value decomposition is the framing contribution, studies are instantiations |
| Methodological musing | One sharply-stated decomposition formula; everything else is empirical |

The long-form draft is *the research diary*; the AAAI version is
*the published paper*. Don't try to fit the diary into 7 pages.

## Hard length targets

- Abstract: 200 words.
- Introduction: 1 page including the decomposition contribution and
  a 4-bullet contribution list.
- Related work: 0.75 page max.
- Method + studies + results: 4 pages.
- Discussion + limitations + threats: 0.75 page.
- References: 1 page.
- Total: 7 pages of body + 1 page of references (the standard
  AAAI main-track length).
- Appendix: up to 2 pages in supplementary, used for raw tables and
  Study-1/2/3/4/5 condensed methods.

## Figures plan

1. **Tool-call timeline** — adoption-over-time across the 5 prompt
   conditions; shows the binary "tool fires" pattern.
2. **Adoption-by-prompt-mode bar chart** — `b` / `vizprimed` /
   `forced` / inline-content adoption rates side by side.
3. **Modality × corpus heatmap** — false-claim catch rates by
   modality (visual / textual / reflection) × corpus (analytic /
   geometric). The headline result.
4. **Inline-content adoption lift** — voluntary adoption with vs.
   without inline-content return. The constructive contribution.

All four are generated by analysis scripts in `experiments/`; the
PDFs go into `paper/aaai27/figures/` and are committed alongside
the .tex.
