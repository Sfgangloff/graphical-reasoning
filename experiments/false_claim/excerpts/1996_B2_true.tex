\paragraph{1996 B--2 \textnormal{[\texttt{asymptotic, integral-inequality}]}}
A reference asserts the following inequality. Determine whether the
assertion is correct.

\textbf{Claim.} For every positive integer $n$,
\[
\left( \frac{2n-1}{e} \right)^{\frac{2n-1}{2}} \;<\;
1 \cdot 3 \cdot 5 \cdots (2n-1) \;<\;
\left( \frac{2n+1}{e} \right)^{\frac{2n+1}{2}}.
\]
